
Trivia Game Specs:

4 categories displayed
User picks a category, is asked a single question for that category
The game tracks what answers the user gets correct and wrong
When game ends, display how many answers were answered correctly and incorrectly 

Questions:

Animal Trivia

What spiny venous fish, common in home aquariums, has become an invasive species in the Caribbean Sea and U.S. Atlantic coastal waters
Lionfish

Vehicle Trivia

The vehicle manufacturer Volvo was founded in what country?
Sweden

History Trivia

On the Apollo 11 moon mission, which astronaut stayed aloft in the command module while Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin walked on the moon?
Michael Collins

Movie Trivia

What car did the character James Bond drive, in the film, Casino Royale?
Aston Martin DBS
